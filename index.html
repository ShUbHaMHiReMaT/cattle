About AVIRA

The Future of Livestock
Health is Here

AVIRA is an intelligent ear tag that continuously monitors vital signs, activity, and behaviour of cattle. Using AI-driven analytics, it predicts diseases up to 48 hours before clinical signs appear, empowering farmers to act early and save their herds.

  • Continuous Bio-monitoring

    Tracks temperature, heart rate, rumination cycles, and motion 24/7 without interrupting natural behaviour.

  • On-Device AI Processing

    Edge AI models analyse sensor data locally, reducing latency and functioning in areas with poor connectivity.

  • Designed for Indian Farmers

    Alerts in regional languages, solar-compatible hardware, and ruggedised for extreme climates and rough terrain.

Process

Up and Running in Minutes

No technical expertise required. AVIRA is designed so any farmer can set it up and start protecting their herd in three simple steps.

01

Attach the AVIRA Ear Tag

Clip the lightweight, IP67-rated AVIRA ear tag onto your cattle's ear just like a conventional identification tag. No tools or training required. The tag activates automatically.

02

Pair with the Mobile App

Download the free AVIRA app, scan the QR code on the tag, and your animal is registered. The tag syncs via Bluetooth and transmits data through cellular or LoRa networks.

03

Receive Health Alerts & Insights

AVIRA's AI engine analyses sensor data continuously. Receive push notifications for anomalies, weekly health summaries, and predictive disease warnings in your local language.
